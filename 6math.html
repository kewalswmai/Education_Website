1. Fill in the blanks: (a) 1 lakh = ………….. ten thousand.
(b) 1 million = ………… hundred thousand.
(c) 1 crore = ………… ten lakh.
(d) 1 crore = ………… million.
(e) 1 million = ………… lakh.
Solutions:
(a) 1 lakh = 10 ten thousand = 1,00,000
(b) 1 million = 10 hundred thousand = 10,00,000
(c) 1 crore = 10 ten lakh = 1,00,00,000
(d) 1 crore = 10 million = 1,00,00,000
(e) 1 million = 10 lakh = 1,000,000
